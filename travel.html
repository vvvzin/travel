Gnocchi al Pomodoro e basilico

  • Time: 50 min
  • Serves: 5 person
Ingredients Quantities
Fresh potatoes 1 kg.
Tomatoes 500 gr.
Basil leaves 50 gr.

Step 1: wash the potatoes and boil them with the peel for at least 20 min.

Step 2: cut the tomoatoes int small dice and dry them until theu become a creamy sauce, than add the basil and switch off the fire.

Step 3: peel the potatoes, smash them and add as much flour required to make the dough smooth

Step 4: shape into strem of dough and cut into small slice

Step 5: throw the gnocchi into boiling water and take them out as soon as they reach the surface of the boiling water

Tiramisu

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 5 person
Ingredients Quantities
eggs 4
Savoiardi biscuits 200 gr
sugar 2 spoons
espresso coffee 4 cups
mascarpone 400 gr.
liquor 2 spoons
cocoa powder 10 grams

Step 1: prepare the coffee possibly italian brand using the italian mocca.

Step 2: beat the eggs yolk with sugar until withening

Step 3: add the mascarpone and beat until creamy

Step 4: beat the white part of the eggs until whitening and add to the mixture

Step 5: add the lequor and mix gently

Step 5: deep the biscuits in the coffee and make a layer of biscuits in the serving dish or single cup

Step 5: cover the dish with the cream and repet the last 2 steps in order to have 4 layers in total

Step 5: cover the last layer with the cocoa powder
